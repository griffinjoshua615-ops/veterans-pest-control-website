Our Promise

  • Veteran‑owned & operated: Our business is founded on military values of service and integrity.
  • Licensed & insured in AL & GA: We hold structural pest control licenses in Alabama and Georgia and carry full insurance for your protection.
  • 17+ years of experience: Owner Eddie Bennett and our team are industry veterans who stay current with the latest pest management techniques.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): We focus on inspection, identification, treatment and prevention to provide long‑lasting solutions.
  • Eco‑friendly options: We offer green treatments and use modern, low‑odor products that are safe for families, pets and the environment.
  • Satisfaction guarantee: If pests return between visits, we’ll retreat the problem areas at no additional cost.
  • 24/7 emergency response: When you need us most, we’re just a call away.

Our Approach

Eliminating pests requires more than just spraying chemicals. Our Integrated Pest Management (IPM) methodology follows a proven process:

Inspect & Identify

We conduct a thorough inspection of your property to identify pest species, harborage areas and contributing conditions.

Customize a Plan

Based on our findings, we design a treatment plan using targeted products, exclusion techniques and sanitation recommendations.

Treat & Protect

Our licensed technicians apply treatments safely and effectively, monitor results and adjust as needed to ensure long‑term control.

Follow Up

After the initial service, we schedule follow‑up visits and provide recommendations to prevent future infestations.
