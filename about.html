Our Story

Veterans Pest Control LLC is a locally owned and operated company based in Alabama. Our founder Eddie Bennett is a U.S. veteran and certified pest control operator with more than 17 years of experience in the industry. Over the years he has earned multiple state licences, completed extensive Integrated Pest Management training and built a team of caring professionals who share his commitment to excellence.

We proudly serve residential, commercial and industrial clients throughout Alabama and neighbouring parts of Georgia. Whether you need an annual termite inspection, emergency bed bug treatment or a comprehensive pest management program for your business, you can count on us for prompt, courteous service and long‑lasting results.
